🏠 After Adoption

Bringing Your Dog Home

Click each step — then interact to learn how to care for your new companion!

🛁

Bath Time First!

Give your new dog a gentle bath as soon as you get home. Use lukewarm water and dog-safe shampoo — rinse thoroughly and be gentle around the face and ears.

🧼 Dog shampoo 🚿 Lukewarm water 🪣 Tub or shower

💡 Talk softly to your dog throughout — it helps them stay calm during their very first bath!

💨

Dry the Fur Completely

After bathing, use a towel to pat dry first, then use a hair dryer on a low-heat setting. Keep the dryer moving and never hold it too close to avoid burns.

🪮 Soft towel 💨 Hair dryer (low heat)

💡 A damp dog left overnight can catch a cold. Make sure the fur is completely dry before bedtime!

🧸

Play & Comfort

Help your dog feel safe and at home with a soft stuffed toy. Playing together builds trust and helps your dog adjust to the new environment faster.

🧸 Stuffed toy / plush 🎾 Chew toy 🪀 Interactive toy

💡 A toy with a familiar scent from the shelter can help reduce separation anxiety on the first night.

🦮

First Walk Together!

Take your dog outside to explore the neighbourhood. The first walk should be short and relaxed — everything is new and exciting for them. Let your dog set the pace.

🦮 Leash & collar 🌳 Quiet outdoor space 💧 Water bottle

💡 Avoid busy roads for the first few walks. A calm environment helps your dog build confidence step by step.
